# Time Series Analysis of UPL Stock Data

## Project Overview

Stock prices exhibit dynamic patterns influenced by market conditions, investor sentiment, and economic factors.

This project performs time series analysis on UPL stock data to understand historical price movements, trends, and forecasting behavior.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze historical stock price trends
- Study market volatility
- Perform time series decomposition
- Identify seasonality and trend components
- Generate stock price forecasts

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains historical stock market information for UPL including:

- Date
- Open Price
- High Price
- Low Price
- Close Price
- Volume

---

## Methodology

1. Data Preprocessing
2. Exploratory Data Analysis
3. Trend Analysis
4. Stationarity Testing
5. Time Series Modeling
6. Forecasting

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Statsmodels
- Scikit-learn

---

## Key Insights

- Historical stock prices show identifiable trend patterns.
- Time series techniques help understand market movements.
- Forecasting models provide estimates of future price behavior.

## Applications

- Investment analysis
- Market trend monitoring
